Vision:

1. Library aims to be one of the best among engineering college libraries in the country.
2. Library intends to incorporate the latest technology and adopt user friendly approach towards student and faculty.
3. Library intends to offer comprehensive services related to dissemination of knowledge.

Mission:

Library serves as a resource center and aims to develop a comprehensive Collection of books and journals useful for faculty and students o the institute and provides an efficient dissemination of knowledge.






Introduction:

DSATM library Started in the year 2011 to serve the information needs of faculty and students community of DSATM. It acts as a centre for the collection of resources predominantly related to all subjects which are in DSATM. It also ensures that information resources are acquired and organized so as to meet the present and future user’s information needs. The main objective of our library is to provide right information to the right users at right time. The library holds a hybrid collection of printed as well as electronic resources which include books, journals, databases, audio-visuals, CDs/DVDs, e-books, e-journals, course materials, previous year question papers, bound volumes, project reports etc

1. Library established in the year 2011
2. Library having 2,581 sq. Mtrs Area
3. Dedicated 50 Computer systems are made available in the Digital Library
4. The Library has a collection of over 23500 volumes, 96 national and international journals, with subscription of e-resources.
5. Private and Discussion rooms are created for R & D work
6. Multi Media resource centre is made available to exhibit student’s talent.

RULES AND REGULATIONS OF THE LIBRARY

1. Users should bring the Smart card compulsorily
2. Swipe the Smart card to open access control door .
3. Leave your bags, handbags and personal belongings at the depository/property counter.
4. Students are prohibited to carry any notebook inside the reference section except loose sheets.
6. No person shall write on, damage or make any mark on any book, journal or other material belongs to the library.
7. Library Documents should be handled with great care.
8. Strict silence should be maintained.
9. Using cell/mobile phones are prohibited.
10. College dress code applies to library also .
11. Eatables are not allowed inside the Library.
12. All users are requested to produce their ID card to the Library staff if required.
