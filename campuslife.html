Campus Life!

DSATM with its heritage has fortified numerous student lives to peaks of success within their 4 years of graduate life. It has a scintillating atmosphere with lush greenery, bringing inspiration and new experience to learn.

Culture, Art & Performances, sports & fitness blend with high academic education brings out the inner potential of the student to the maximum.

Esperanza

DAYANANDA SAGAR ACADEMY OF TECHNOLOGY AND MANAGEMENT has been conducting ESPERANZA , A Techno cultural Inter College Fest Every year , with abundance of talent and skills of the students exposed to various competitions and big prizes . The last edition of the fest featured major celebrities like Mr. Kenny Sebastian and Mr. Sanjith Hegde. It has been a hugely succesful event that encourages a lot of talent and leadership amongst the students and staff.

Arts And Culture

Culture plays an important role in the development of DSI. It represents a set of shared attitudes, values, goals and practices. Culture and creativity manifest themselves in almost all economic, social and other activities. DSATM is symbolized by the plurality of its culture.
