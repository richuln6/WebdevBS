About The Department

Intake of the Department = 120
The Department of Computer Science and Engineering at DSATM offers undergraduate and doctoral programs. Approved by AICTE and affiliated to VTU, Belgaum, the department comprises of faculty with a range of research interests and a dedication to teach

Vision

To be an excellent center for learning technology knowledge with a focus on research and development imparting professional education in Civil Engineering for ever changing societal needs with Credibility, Integrity and Ethical Standards

Mission

M1 : To achieve commendable success and academic excellence in Circular, Co-curricular and Extra-curricular activities. M2 : To face the challenges of construction industry for sustainable growth using inclusive Technologies. M3 : To strive for quality in infrastructural development by exercising quality checks in accordance with codal provisions and by-laws M4 : To discharge duties with Environmental, Economic and Social Considerations. M5 : To enable the graduates to use the available tools and technologies to Assess, Analyse and Design Civil Engineering Structures

Program Educational Objectives (PEO's):

PEO1 :To equip graduates with fundamental knowledge necessary for a successful career in diverse field of Civil Engineering PEO2 :To empower graduates in Research Methodologies, Problem Solving, Innovative and Multiple Thinking in multidisciplinary domains of Civil Engineering PEO3 :Enable graduates to pursue higher education and research in the field of Civil Engineering, and engage in the process of life-long learning. PEO4 :To perform Social and Professional responsibilities in Ethical manner to serve the society.

Program Outcomes (PO's) based on NBA graduate attributes :

PO 1:Engineering Knowledge:Apply the knowledge of mathematics, science, engineering fundamentals, and an engineering specialization to the solution of complex engineering problems. PO 2:Problem Analysis:Identify, formulate, review research literature, and analyze complex engineering problems reaching substantiated conclusions using first principles of mathematics, natural sciences, and engineering sciences. PO 3:Design/Development of solutions:Design solutions for complex engineering problems and design system components or processes that meet the specified needs with appropriate consideration for the public health and safety, and the cultural, societal, and environmental considerations. PO 4:Conduct investigations of complex problems:Use research-based knowledge and research methods including design of experiments, analysis and interpretation of data, and synthesis of the information to provide valid conclusions. PO 5:Modern Tool Usage:Create, select, and apply appropriate techniques, resources, and modern engineering and IT tools including prediction and modeling to complex engineering activities with an understanding of the limitations. PO 6:Apply reasoning informed by the contextual knowledge to assess societal, health, safety, legal and cultural issues and the consequent responsibilities relevant to the professional engineering practice. PO 7:Understand the impact of the professional engineering solutions in societal and environmental contexts, and demonstrate the knowledge of, and need for sustainable development. PO 8:Ethics:Apply ethical principles and commit to professional ethics and responsibilities and norms of the engineering practice. PO 9:Individual and Team Work:: Function effectively as an individual, and as a member or leader in diverse teams, and in multidisciplinary settings. PO 10:Communication:Communicate effectively on complex engineering activities with the engineeringcommunity and with society at large, such as, being able to comprehend and write effective reports and design documentation, make effective presentations, and give and receive clear instructions. PO 11:Project Management and Finance:Demonstrate knowledge and understanding of the engineering and management principles and apply these to one’s own work, as a member and leader in a team, to manage projects and in multidisciplinary environments. PO 12:Life-Long Learning:Recognize the need for, and have the preparation and ability to engage in independent and life-long learning in the broadest context of technological change.

About The Course :

The Department of Civil Engineering is headed by Dr. K N Vishwanath, B.E, M.Tech, PhD. Department of Civil Engineering is in the 4th year of its existence. Since its inception in the year 2012, the Department has seen great progress in the field of Civil Engineering both Professionally and Academics. The department has well qualified, trained and dedicated staff who constantly strive for the all-around development of the students. The department is well equipped with excellent and world class infrastructure which helps students to bridge the gap between the academics and industry. The department of Civil Engineering is associated with ICI-KB center and MoU’s with Edu CADD. The course aims to impart quality education to students, to bring about the skills and attributes in the field of Planning, Designing and Estimating various Civil Engineering Structures with a focus on Environmental and Sustainable development.
